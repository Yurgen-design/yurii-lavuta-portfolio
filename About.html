Mission Statement

Bridging the gap between design and development by combining logical engineering with human-centric empathy.

With over 3 years of commercial experience, I specialize in the end-to-end product design cycle — from in-depth user research and strategy to the final UI. My approach is rooted in lean methodology: I am accustomed to working in small, cross-functional agile teams where speed, flexibility, and communication are paramount. A key focus of mine is SaaS and B2B solutions.

I don't limit myself to just the visual aspect. Leveraging my skills in Framer and Webflow, along with an understanding of HTML/CSS/JS (in synergy with AI), I can independently build and launch websites. Furthermore, following my advanced professional training in Germany, I am well-versed in German and European data security standards and accessibility guidelines, enabling me to design products that meet strict technical requirements.
